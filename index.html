About Our College

Butwal Multiple Campus (BMC) is a renowned constituent campus of Tribhuvan University, located in Butwal, Rupandehi, Nepal. Established over 47 years ago with the initial aim of training primary school teachers, the campus has grown into a major academic institution outside the Kathmandu Valley. BMC offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various faculties, including Management, Humanities and Social Sciences, Education, Science and Technology, and Law. The Management faculty provides programs such as Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS), Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), and Master of Business Studies (MBS). The Humanities and Social Sciences faculty offers Bachelor of Arts (BA) degrees in subjects like Rural Development and Sociology, along with Master of Arts (MA) degrees in English, Nepali, and Economics. The Education faculty includes Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) and Master of Education (M.Ed.) programs with specializations in Nepali, Health, English, Mathematics, and Educational Planning and Management (EPM). The Science and Technology faculty features a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (B.Sc. CSIT), as well as Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) programs in Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology, Statistics, Earth Hazard, Environmental Science, and Mathematics. The Law faculty offers Bachelor of Laws (LLB) and Bachelor of Arts Bachelor of Laws (BALLB) degrees. BMC is situated on hilly terrain near the Golpark area of Butwal Municipality, providing a peaceful and conducive learning environment. It has a reputation for academic excellence and attracts students from various districts across Nepal. The campus is well-equipped with educational facilities and has dedicated faculty members committed to providing quality education. For more details, the campus can be contacted at 071-540134 or 071-551516, and inquiries can be sent via email to butwalmcampus@gmail.com. With its long-standing commitment to higher education, Butwal Multiple Campus continues to be a significant educational hub in western Nepal.
