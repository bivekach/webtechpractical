About Our College

Butwal Multiple Campus is one of the leading educational institutions in Nepal, providing a wide range of academic programs under the faculties of Science, Management, Humanities, and Education. Affiliated with Tribhuvan University, the campus offers undergraduate and graduate courses, including BSc. CSIT, BBA, BALLB, BSc in General Science, and BA in Humanities. With a strong focus on academic excellence, Butwal Multiple Campus is equipped with experienced faculty members, well-structured curricula, and modern facilities to enhance student learning. The institution is committed to fostering a dynamic educational environment that promotes research, innovation, and practical knowledge.

Located in Butwal, the campus serves as a hub for higher education, attracting students from different regions. Its mission is to provide quality education that prepares students for successful careers in various fields, making it a preferred choice for higher studies in Nepal.


For more details, the campus can be contacted at 071-540134 or 071-551516, and inquiries can be sent via email to butwalmcampus@gmail.com. With its long-standing commitment to higher education, Butwal Multiple Campus continues to be a significant educational hub in western Nepal.
